🎒 Go Bag Essentials

  • Water (3 days minimum per person)
  • Non-perishable food & snacks
  • First aid kit + medications
  • Flashlight + spare batteries
  • Phone charger & power bank
  • Important documents (waterproof)
  • Warm clothes & rain jacket
  • Glasses / contact lenses

🔥 Fire Emergency

  1. Get out immediately
  2. Stay low to avoid smoke
  3. Call 111 once safe
  4. Do NOT go back inside
  5. Alert others

🌍 Earthquake

  1. Drop, Cover, Hold On
  2. Stay inside until shaking stops
  3. Move away from windows
  4. Check injuries after
  5. Expect aftershocks

🌊 Flood

  1. Move to higher ground
  2. Avoid flood water
  3. Turn off electricity if safe
  4. Follow official orders

🌋 Volcanic Eruption

  1. Stay indoors with windows closed
  2. Wear mask if outside
  3. Avoid ash areas
  4. Follow Civil Defence
