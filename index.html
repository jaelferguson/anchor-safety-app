🎒 Go Bag Essentials

  • Water (3 days)
  • Non-perishable food
  • First aid kit
  • Flashlight + batteries
  • Phone charger / power bank
  • Important documents
  • Warm clothes

🔥 Fire Emergency

  1. Get out immediately
  2. Stay low (avoid smoke)
  3. Call 111
  4. Do NOT go back inside

🌍 Earthquake

  1. Drop, Cover, Hold
  2. Stay inside
  3. Move away from windows
  4. Check injuries after

🌊 Flood

  1. Move to higher ground
  2. Avoid flood water
  3. Turn off electricity
  4. Follow official alerts

🌋 Volcanic Eruption

  1. Stay indoors
  2. Wear mask if outside
  3. Avoid ash exposure
  4. Follow evacuation orders
